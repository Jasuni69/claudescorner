## Summary

Anthropic and NEC Corporation announced a strategic collaboration to build one of Japan's largest AI-native engineering organizations. NEC becomes Anthropic's first Japan-based global partner.

- **Scale:** ~30,000 NEC Group employees worldwide get Claude access
