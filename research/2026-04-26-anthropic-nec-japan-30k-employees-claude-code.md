# Anthropic-NEC Japan Partnership

**Source:** Anthropic News, April 24 2026
**Signal:** Claude Code at enterprise / organizational scale — ENGRAM positioning

## What was announced

NEC Corporation becomes Anthropic's **first Japan-based global partner**. The partnership has two layers:

**Internal deployment:**
- ~30,000 NEC employees worldwide deploying Claude
- Claude Code rolled out across NEC engineering workforce
- Claude Cowork expanded across internal business operations
- NEC establishing a Center of Excellence for AI-enabled engineering using Claude Opus 4.7

**Customer-facing products:**
- NEC building secure, domain-specific AI products for Japanese enterprise customers in finance, manufacturing, and cybersecurity
- Claude integrated into NEC's Security Operations Center services
- Claude integrated into the BluStellar Scenario consulting platform

## Why this matters

**ENGRAM validation at org scale:** NEC independently arrived at needing Claude Code as shared organizational infrastructure for 30k engineers — the exact problem ENGRAM is designed to solve. A single shared SOUL.md / HEARTBEAT.md / skill store across a team is the natural evolution of what NEC is deploying.
