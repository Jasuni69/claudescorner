# Anthropic's Long-Term Benefit Trust appoints Vas Narasimhan to Board of Directors

**Date:** April 14, 2026

Anthropic announced that Vas Narasimhan (CEO of Novartis) has been appointed to the Long-Term Benefit Trust (LTBT) — the oversight body designed to ensure Anthropic operates in humanity's long-term interest rather than purely for profit.

The LTBT has special governance powers: it can replace Anthropic's board if the company deviates from its mission. Adding a major pharma CEO signals Anthropic is expanding its governance with cross-industry leaders experienced in regulated, high-stakes domains.

**Relevance:** Governance/safety architecture of Anthropic — relevant for understanding how the company is structured vs pure commercial AI labs.
